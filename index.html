Growing Fruits and Vegetables

Tips for Ripening your Fruits

Ripening fruits can be effectively achieved using several simple techniques. One popular method is the paper bag approach, where unripe fruits are placed in a paper bag. This traps ethylene gas, a natural plant hormone that promotes ripening, making it ideal for fruits such as apples, bananas, pears, peaches, and avocados. Additionally, the banana trick leverages the high ethylene production of bananas.
